  • "Some day my prince will come"

This fairytale Enchanted is basically about a princess named “Giselle” where she is banished from her kingdom by her lover’s stepmom and eventually, she’s transported to the real world where she meets a lawyer and falls in love with her. However, in this story written by Billy Kelly there is part where her step mom “Narissa” comes to the real world to kill Giselle where she offers the last poisoned apple to Giselle promising “sweet dreams and happy ending”. As soon as she takes a bite of the apple she falls unconsciously where true loves kiss would only break the poisoned apple’s spell. Edward’s (prince from the animated world) kiss fails to awake Giselle but when Robert (the lawyer she meets in the real world) kisses her, Giselle awakens. So accordingly, the overall idea of the tale Enchanted would be ‘Love’ is dynamic and it requires action to thrive and it also includes actions that express love, affection, compassion, respect and kindness
